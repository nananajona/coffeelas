How To Use?

4 Easy Steps

  • Step 1: Scoop your coffee powder into the capsule
  • Step 2: Flatten the powder with a tamper or the back of your scoop
  • Step 3: Place the capsule into your coffee machine
  • Step 4: Set your desired settings and your hot cuppa's done!
