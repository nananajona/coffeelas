Our Story

New here?
Let us tell you why you should stay.

As the global market for coffee capsules grows, so does the waste it produces. Annually, the global footprint of coffee capsule waste is about 576,000 metric tones = 4,400 school buses. Hence, we are on a mission. We are not just another coffee company. We are THE sustainable coffee company.

Who says enjoying delicious coffee whilst being eco-friendly is hard? Over here, we have created sustainable, affordable, and compatible coffee capsules.

The best part? Enjoy your coffee in the comfort of your homes.
